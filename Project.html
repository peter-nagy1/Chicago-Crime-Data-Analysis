Chicago crimes 2001-2018¶

Crimes that occurred in the City of Chicago from 2001, up until mid Nov. 2018

Description¶

This dataset reflects reported incidents of crime (with the exception of murders where data exists for each victim) that occurred in the City of Chicago from 2001 up until approximately half of November 2018.

Source: Chicago Data Portal

Data quality¶

The data source presents multiple crimes that occured in Chicago between 2001 and 2018. The crimes are described and located by the block, by a general location (street, apartment, sidewalk, ...), by the Chicago district number and by the Chicago community area number. Every crime is labeled by a case number, which makes it easy to look up for further information. Finally, every crime mentions if the suspect was arrested and if it was a domestic crime.

It's relevant to data science, since we, as data scientists, can calculate and come up with a lot of conclusions concerning crime in Chicago or crime in general as well. For example, which areas in Chicago are the safest and which ones are the riskiest, what location (street, sidewalk, ...) should people avoid to remain safe and many more.
